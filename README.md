# Swiggy Data Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze **Swiggy food delivery data** to understand sales performance, customer satisfaction, and ordering behavior across different time periods and locations. This dashboard helps stakeholders track key business metrics, identify high-performing regions and cities, and uncover trends in food preferences to support strategic business decisions.

* <a href="https://github.com/indradityaa/Swiggy-Data-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Swiggy%20Raw%20Data%20Excel.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)

### Business Requirement Document (BRD): KPIs

* **Total Sales (₹):** Overall revenue generated from food orders.
* **Average Rating:** Measures customer satisfaction across all restaurants.
* **Average Order Value (₹):** Average revenue generated per order.
* **Ratings Count:** Total number of customer reviews received.
* **Total Orders:** Total number of food orders placed.

### BRD: Charts & Analysis

* **Monthly Sales Trend:** Analyzes how total sales change month by month.
* **Daily Sales Trend:** Shows sales and order variations across days of the week.
* **Total Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg):** Compares revenue contribution by food category.
* **Total Sales by State (Map Visualization):** Displays state-wise sales distribution.
* **Quarterly Performance Summary:** Combined analysis of sales, ratings, and orders by quarter.
* **Top 5 Cities by Sales:** Identifies cities contributing the highest revenue.
* **Weekly Trend Analysis:** Examines weekly sales patterns to identify peak and low-performing periods.

## Process

* Reviewed and cleaned the dataset by handling missing values and correcting inconsistencies.
* Standardized date, numerical, and categorical fields for accurate analysis.
* Created calculated fields for total sales, average order value, and performance metrics.
* Built pivot tables to analyze KPIs across time, location, and food type.
* Designed an interactive Excel dashboard integrating all charts and KPIs.
* Added slicers and filters to enable dynamic analysis by time period and region.

## Project Insights

* Sales trends vary significantly across months, weeks, and days of the week.
* Certain states and cities contribute a major share of total revenue.
* Veg and Non-Veg food categories show different revenue contribution patterns.
* Quarterly analysis provides a clear comparison of overall business performance.
* Customer ratings and order volume help evaluate service quality and demand.

This Swiggy data analysis dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer satisfaction, and ordering trends.
